STATEMENT OF WORK

This Statement of Work is issued under and governed by the Master Framework Agreement dated November 20, 2025, by and between Larkspur Cloud Systems B.V. ("Provider") and Dunmore Industrial Group ("Customer").

1. SCOPE
Provider will license its inventory-optimization module to Customer for use across Customer's European operations.

2. PRICING
License fees are tiered based on the number of active named licenses, denominated in Euros:

- 1 to 200 licenses: EUR 65.00 per license per month
- 201 to 750 licenses: EUR 54.00 per license per month
- 751 to 1,500 licenses: EUR 46.00 per license per month

3. TERM
This SOW is effective for twelve (12) months from the date last signed and does not include a renewal mechanism; a successor SOW must be executed for continued use.

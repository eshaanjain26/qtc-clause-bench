MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made as of February 5, 2026, between Copperline Payments Inc. ("Provider") and Wrenfield Retail Holdings ("Customer").

1. SERVICES
Provider will supply payment reconciliation services described in Order Forms executed under this Agreement.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall have a term of six (6) months from the Effective Date. This Agreement shall terminate automatically at the end of that six-month term and shall not renew, whether automatically or otherwise. Continued services after the term requires a new, separately negotiated agreement.

3. FEES
Fees are set forth in the applicable Order Form and are not tiered; a single flat monthly fee applies regardless of transaction volume.

4. WARRANTY
Provider warrants that services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.

MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of January 12, 2026, between Thistledown API Services Inc. ("Provider") and Marrow Financial Technologies ("Customer").

1. SERVICES
Provider will make its API platform available to Customer for the transaction volumes described below.

2. PRICING
The base list price per API call is tiered based on monthly call volume, as follows:

- 0 to 1,000,000 calls: $0.0020 per call
- 1,000,001 to 10,000,000 calls: $0.0015 per call
- 10,000,001+ calls: $0.0010 per call

Tier pricing applies on a marginal basis to calls within each band for the applicable month.

3. ANNUAL SPEND DISCOUNT
In addition to tiered list pricing, where Customer commits in writing to a minimum annual spend of one million dollars ($1,000,000) across the contract year, Provider shall apply an additional discount of twelve percent (12%) off the tier pricing otherwise calculated under Section 2 for that contract year.

4. TERM
This Agreement has an Initial Term of twelve (12) months and does not include any renewal or extension mechanism; the parties must execute a new agreement for continued services beyond the Initial Term.

5. DATA SECURITY
Provider shall maintain commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards for Customer data.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.

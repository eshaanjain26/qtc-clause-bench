STATEMENT OF WORK

Customer: Redcliff Municipal Water Authority
Provider: Thistledown API Services Inc.

1. SCOPE OF WORK
Provider will conduct a technical assessment of Customer's existing telemetry infrastructure and deliver a written findings report within thirty (30) days of the kickoff meeting.

2. DELIVERABLES
- Kickoff meeting notes
- Draft findings report
- Final findings report incorporating Customer feedback

3. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
Customer will provide Provider with read access to relevant telemetry dashboards and designate a single point of contact for the duration of the engagement.

4. ACCEPTANCE
The final findings report shall be deemed accepted ten (10) business days after delivery unless Customer provides written objections specifying deficiencies.

STATEMENT OF WORK

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is issued under Exhibit C to the Master Services Agreement between Cascadia Cloud Services LLC and Fernway Logistics Group (the "MSA"). This SOW incorporates the MSA's general terms by reference; in the event of conflict specific to the equipment described herein, this SOW controls.

1. SCOPE
Provider will supply and configure edge-compute appliances at Customer's regional distribution centers.

2. PRICING
Appliance pricing is tiered by total unit quantity ordered under this SOW:

- 1 to 10 units: $12,000 per unit
- 11 to 25 units: $10,500 per unit
- 26 to 50 units: $9,000 per unit

3. PREPAYMENT DISCOUNT
Customer may elect to prepay the full SOW value within fifteen (15) days of the SOW effective date. Orders paid in full within that window receive a five percent (5%) discount off the total invoice, applied as a one-time credit; this discount applies only to this single purchase and does not affect future orders.

4. DELIVERY
Provider shall deliver ordered appliances within sixty (60) days of order confirmation.
